Our Philosophy

Crafted with Intention.
Chosen with Care.

At Le Rouge, we believe the best bottles are the ones with a story worth sharing. Our team of seasoned sommeliers travels the world's finest wine regions — from the limestone plateaus of Burgundy to the sun-drenched valleys of Mendoza — to bring you selections that speak of terroir, craft, and character.

With two boutiques in the heart of Singapore's CBD, we are your trusted guide through the world of fine wine and premium spirits. Whether you're seeking a quiet evening red, a celebratory champagne, or the perfect gift, Le Rouge has the answer.
